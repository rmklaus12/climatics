Our Services

Hydronic Heating

Radiators or Underfloor – Hydronic heating is the most energy efficient, safe and hygienic way to heat your whole home. It offers comfortable warmth while saving you money on your energy bills at the same time as reducing your carbon footprint. Hydronic Heating is an energy efficient, dust free, healthy, quiet, and reliable heat.

Air Conditioning

Climatics offer a range of the latest refrigerated A/C split systems that utilise “inverter technology”. The inverter is a compressor that fluctuates its speed as required to meet the A/C requirements, while saving you on energy costs. Reverse Cycle A/C offers powerful, energy efficient heating and cooling to ensure uninterrupted comfort.

Gas Ducted Heating

Gas ducted heating has been heating Melbourne homes for generations. Climatics offers a range of the latest ducted heating systems that will save you on energy. Gas Ducted Heating offers an affordable and reliable heating solution for your home.

Ventilation

To ensure the air you breathe is high quality and free from moisture, odours, smoke, heat, dust, airborne bacteria and carbon dioxide. Climatics offer a range of mechanical ventilation systems for domestic and commercial buildings. Hygienic, clean, fresh, reliable.
